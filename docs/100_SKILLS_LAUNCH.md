# 100 Skills Launch Note

Learn Anything Skills now ships 100 beginner-first skills.

The goal is not to dump prompts. Each skill follows the same learning contract:

- next action
- visible artifact
- check question
- what to ignore
- resource cap
- reflection

## Suggested Post

I pushed Learn Anything Skills to 100 skills.

It now covers self-learning loops for:

- AI research and papers
- embodied AI and robotics
- math and CS foundations
- daily learning operations
- writing, teaching, and portfolio outputs
- PhD and career research workflows
- tools, data, debugging, APIs, and codebases
- trust, verification, safety, and metacognition

The point is simple: when you want to learn a field like embodied AI, the agent should not just answer. It should help you decide what to ignore, what to do today, what artifact to produce, and how to test whether you understood it.
