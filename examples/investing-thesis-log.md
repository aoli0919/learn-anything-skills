# Example: Investing Thesis Learning Loop

## Input

```text
$learning-compass I want to understand semiconductor stocks.
I know basic investing words but keep getting distracted by news.
```

## Output Sketch

### North Star

In 30 days, write a one-page beginner thesis for one semiconductor company, including business model, demand drivers, risks, and one bear case.

### Week 1

Learn the supply chain:

- designers
- foundries
- equipment
- memory
- packaging

Visible artifact:

- one supply-chain map

### Week 2

Pick one company and build a vocabulary sheet:

- revenue segments
- customers
- margins
- cycle risk

### Week 3

Read two earnings call excerpts, not ten news articles.

Resource cap:

- one annual report section
- two earnings call excerpts
- one industry primer

### Week 4

Write the thesis and one bear case.

### Check Question

What would have to be true for your thesis to be wrong?

### Reflection

If you cannot name the bear case, you are not done learning yet.
